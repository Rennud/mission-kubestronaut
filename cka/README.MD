## üìñ About the Exam

The purpose of the **CKA** program is to provide assurance that CKAs have the skills, knowledge, and competency to deploy, manage, and troubleshoot Kubernetes clusters effectively.  

**Exam Details:**
- **Format:** Performance-Based Exam  
- **Duration:** 120 minutes  
- **Passing Score:** 66%
